How to Download YouTube Thumbnails on iPhone (Step by Step)

Step 1: Copy the YouTube Video URL

Open the YouTube app on your iPhone and find the video whose thumbnail you want to download. There are two ways to copy the URL:

Troubleshooting Common Issues on iPhone

Having trouble saving a YouTube thumbnail on your iPhone? Here are quick fixes for the most common problems:

  • Save to Photos option is not appearing - Make sure you are long-pressing the actual thumbnail image, not the download button text. If it still does not appear, tap the download button first so the image opens in a new tab, then long-press it there.
  • The URL is not fetching a thumbnail - Make sure you copied a video URL and not a channel URL, playlist URL, or YouTube homepage link. The URL must contain a video ID to work.
  • Image saved but cannot find it - Check the Recents album in your Photos app. Downloaded images always appear there sorted by the date they were saved.
  • Safari is not downloading the file - Go to iPhone Settings, scroll down to Safari, tap Downloads, and make sure it is set to save to Photos or iCloud Drive.
  • Image quality looks low - Make sure you tapped the Full HD option and not the SD or HD option. Full HD at 1280x720 is the highest quality available for most YouTube videos.
