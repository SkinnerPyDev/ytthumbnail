Your privacy matters. We do not collect, store, or track any personal data.

Last updated: 24 February 2026

Key Point All processing happens directly on your device. We never see, store, or transmit your YouTube URLs, thumbnails, or any personal information.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how downloadythumbnail.online handles information when you use our YouTube thumbnail, logo, and banner downloader tools. By accessing this site, you agree to the practices outlined below.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We take a zero-data approach. We do not collect or store:

  • Personal data (name, email, location, etc.)
  • YouTube video URLs, video IDs, or channel URLs you paste
  • Downloaded thumbnails, logos, banners, or any extracted media
  • IP addresses, browsing history, or device fingerprints

Everything happens client-side in your browser. Once you close the tab, all data is gone.

3. Cookies & Tracking

Our website does not use cookies for tracking purposes. We do not use advertising trackers, retargeting pixels, or behavior monitoring scripts. We use Google Analytics solely to understand aggregate traffic patterns (page views, device types) without tracking individual users.

4. Third-Party Services

Our tools interact with YouTube's public infrastructure to retrieve publicly available thumbnail images. We do not access private data, require YouTube login, or use YouTube API keys that track individual usage. We do not store, modify, or republish any third-party content.

5. Data Security

Since we operate on a zero-data model, there is no user data to protect, leak, or breach. All tool operations run securely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No server-side processing of your content occurs.

6. External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (YouTube, social media, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices, terms, or content of those external websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Since we collect no data at all, this concern does not apply to our service.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with the updated date shown above.
